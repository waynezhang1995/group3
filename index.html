Client

Our client is the St. Jude's Anglican Home, a nursing home for elderly citizens in Vancouver. It features 2 floors with a total of 55 beds in 45 rooms, including a couple private rooms. It is a non profit nursing home primarily funded by Vancouver Coastal Health, but user fees and donations also go towards taking care of the home and its citizens. St. Jude's Anglican Home has existed since 1948, evolving with the years and is now a successful modern nursing home fit for any elderly citizen to stay, with citizens requiring more specialized care to be admitted into "Extended Care" for more detailed care.

St. Jude's Anglican Home's Request

St. Jude's Anglican Home has requested the help of our company to assist them in creating and designing a system that will help them ensure patient safety upon delivering food. Over the years, the nursing staff have become familiar with the patients in the nursing home, and are familiar with what each patient is capable of. Unfortunately, when a regular staff member goes on leave, they are replaced with a casual nurse, who may not know this information, leading to potentially dangerous situations regarding allergies and ability to chew and swallow. Thus, St. Jude's has asked us to think of a method or system to be implemented to make sure that all nurses who deliver food are aware of every patient's personal and medical needs, regardless of whether or not they are familiar with the patient. This will allow them to ensure the utmost quality when it comes to patient safety.
